what the power law?

A functional relationship between two quantities where a relative change in one results in a proportional change in the other. Mathematically, given a relation \(f(x) = ax^{-k}\), \[ f(cx) = a(cx)^{-k} = c^{-k}f(x) \\prop f(x) \]
